Privacy Policy for OneTap

Last Updated: February 27, 2026

Your Privacy Matters — OneTap is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share your personal information.

Information We Collect

OneTap operates entirely on your device and does not collect or transmit personal information to external servers. The app may access:

  • Clipboard Data — Only when you explicitly use the app to download videos from URLs copied to your clipboard
  • Storage — To save downloaded videos to your device
  • Network Access — To download videos from supported platforms

How We Use Information

All data processing happens locally on your device:

  • URLs are processed only to facilitate video downloads
  • Download history is stored locally on your device
  • No user data is transmitted to our servers or third parties

Data Storage

OneTap stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Download history and preferences
  • App settings and configurations
  • Downloaded video files in your chosen location

You can clear this data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data from your device settings.

Third-Party Services

OneTap may interact with third-party video platforms to download content. We do not control the privacy practices of these platforms. Please review their respective privacy policies:

  • Video downloads are performed directly from the source platforms
  • We do not share your information with these platforms beyond what's necessary for the download

Permissions

OneTap requests the following permissions:

  • Internet Access — Required to download videos
  • Storage Access — Required to save downloaded videos
  • Foreground Service — To show download progress and manage downloads
  • System Alert Window — For the floating bubble interface (optional)

Children's Privacy

OneTap is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Security

Since all data is processed and stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security measures. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Using device encryption and lock screen protection
  • Only downloading content from trusted sources

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and delete your local data by clearing app data
  • Revoke permissions at any time through your device settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all associated data
